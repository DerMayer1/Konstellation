# Frontend Terminal Design Direction

The Constellation web UI uses a legacy financial terminal visual language.

## Principles

- Dense information layout over spacious marketing UI.
- Monospace typography for all chrome, labels, tables and controls.
- Black/navy surfaces with electric blue structural bars.
- Amber and yellow for headers, labels and important forecast values.
- Green for positive revenue signals.
- Red for risk, slippage and negative forecast signals.
- Rectangular panels with hard borders; avoid soft SaaS cards.
- Demo data must remain visibly marked as synthetic.

## Guardrails

- Do not use Bloomberg branding, logos or product names.
- Do not hide real-data requirements behind demo fixtures.
- Do not introduce decorative gradients, rounded card stacks or pastel palettes.
- Keep navigation and controls compact, keyboard-visible and data-tool oriented.
